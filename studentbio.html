Student Bio

Nicholas Bernhard

Nicholas Bernhard is an author, composer, and filmmaker. He directed and scored the acclaimed documentary Blackstone's Equation: The Tim Masters Story, about Colorado's most notorious wrongful conviction. He writes the column Tales of the Northern Coal Field for Yellow Scene Magazine, and wrote the novella November in America, about the Coal Strike of 1927.

Mr. Bernhard lives in Colorado.
